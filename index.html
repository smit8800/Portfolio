About Me

I am a dedicated Machine Learning enthusiast currently pursuing my Master of Science in Computer Science at Stony Brook University. With a strong foundation in advanced topics like Computer Vision and Machine Learning, I have a GPA of 3.78. In my role as a Research Project Assistant at Stony Brook University, I focused on 3D CT image generation and segmentation. I implemented state-of-the-art techniques to enhance CT Segmentation Map Generation. Previously, at SigmaRed Technologies, I developed an end-to-end ML monitoring platform, detecting real-time data drift and anomalies. I excelled in deploying advanced drift detection algorithms, bolstering model performance. My technical expertise spans Python, C/C++, Go, and frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch. I am adept at handling large datasets and have a strong background in various libraries and frameworks. With a track record of impactful research projects and a solid academic foundation, I am eager to apply my skills to create innovative Machine Learning solutions.
